About 10 things you won't be taught in school

Don't let schooling interfere with your Education - Mark Twain. Schooling is a good foundation for success but there are experiences, skill sets, etc, which are more important and mandatory as they can decide either your success or failure. It happened that our traditional schools don't cover those experiences in their curriculum. Therefore let's expand the four walls of our classroom and get new insights.

Met our Impactful speakers

We are going to be learning lifelong skill sets and Knowledge from this program. Therefore we are glad to be having seasoned S.U.G( Student Union Government) leaders from various capacities to teach and share their stories, experiences, etc, in areas of Education and life worth knowing.

Ogwuru Prince George

Secretary General, SUG (MOUAU)

He is from Abia state in Bende LGA Ozuitem and Igbere and a student of the Michael okpara University of Agriculture umudike currently in 400l. He obtained his NCE from Abia state college of Education Technical Arochukwu in Biology and chemistry before proceeding to MOUAU to obtain his degree in Agriculture specializing in plant pathology.

He is a born-again Christain from the winners chapel and had undergone the Bible School training BCC, LCC and LDC. Prince George runs a foundation known as the Prince George foundation which he uses to reach out to the Girl child for the ladies and Save the next generation for the men. He runs an online program on wealth creation in Agriculture which has imparted hundreds of both graduates and undergraduates. He has served in various capacities in the school as an SRC Hon member, Secretary on Religious matters currently serving as SUG SECRETARY GENERAL and aspiring for SUG president come to the next election. In summary prince, George is an Educationist, Agriculturist, Realtor and lover of Christ and humanity.

Great Don Sharon Ayomide

President, SUG (TASUED)

He is known for his passion to make an impact in the world A skillful content writer and Professional Sales and Marketing Agent who has proven to possess time management and productivity skills.

Great Don Sharon Ayomide who is a Media Influencer believes service to humanity is the best service of life and for this reason became a unionist on campus, he has served as the General Governor of his College, The Welfare Director and he has had the privilege to serve as The 14th Student Union President of his outstanding institution the Tai Solarin University of Education. Currently in his final years studying Educational Management and Sociology, he has received numerous awards for his excellence and contribution to the peaceful coexistence of students in his institution. Great Don Sharon Ayomide is the founder of Triangle Praise Crew and Great Minds Foundation. The Triangle Praise Crew is a team of young and vibrant members who have willingly dedicated their time to contribute meaningfully to the kingdom of God. The Great Minds Foundation is an academic forum that specializes in training students to have academic excellence, they also inspire students to become the best in life. Great Don Sharon Ayomide is a brand ambassador to some notable companies in the country like NLARGE MEDIA and DAGWOF. Great Don Sharon Ayomide sees himself as a visionary leader and wishes to attain more greatness in life.

Abdulyekinn Uthman Odunayo

National President, NOUN.
