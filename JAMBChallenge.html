About Jambites Awareness Program

JAP is an annual FREE program hosted by Impactful School for secondary graduates and students looking to further their education in higher institutions. We have been through the system, and we teach you the other side of learning that is not taught in class but is essential to your success in the higher institution.

This year JAP 2.0 is focused on uncovering and teaching you the skillsets to thrive on campus


  • The secrets the higher institutions won't tell you.
  • Career Awareness survey
  • The secrets of making high-value friendships.
  • Giveway sessions
  • Jamb tutorial scholarship.
  • And lots more...
